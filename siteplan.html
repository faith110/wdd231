FitHer Journey Site Plan

Site Name

FitHer Journey

This name was selected because it represents a fitness website designed specifically for women who want to improve their health, build strength, grow their glutes, and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Site Purpose

The website provides workout guides, fitness tips, nutrition advice, and beginner-friendly resources for women interested in strength training, glute growth, weight management, and overall wellness.

Scenarios

  • What exercises are most effective for growing my glutes?
  • What foods should I eat to support muscle growth and recovery?

Color Scheme

```

Deep Purple (#6A0DAD) - Used for headings, navigation, buttons, and footer sections.

Soft Pink (#F8BBD0) - Used for page backgrounds, accents, and call-to-action areas.

```

Typography

```

Poppins - Used for page headings and navigation.

Open Sans - Used for body text, articles, forms, and general content.
