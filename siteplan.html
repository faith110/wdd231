FitHer Journey Site Plan

Site Name

FitHer Journey

This name was selected because it represents a fitness website designed specifically for women who want to improve their health, build strength, and achieve their fitness goals. The word "Journey" emphasizes long-term progress and growth.

Site Purpose

The website provides fitness information and resources for women with a focus on glute growth, strength training, nutrition, and healthy lifestyle habits. Visitors can learn workout routines, proper exercise techniques, meal planning, and fitness tips for beginners.

Scenarios

  • What exercises are most effective for growing my glutes?
  • What foods should I eat to support muscle growth and recovery?
  • Can I build glutes at home without gym equipment?
  • How many times per week should I train my glutes?

Color Scheme

```

Deep Purple (#6A0DAD) - Used for headings, navigation bar, buttons, and footer.

Soft Pink (#F8BBD0) - Used for accents, highlights, call-to-action buttons, and decorative elements.

```

Typography

```

Poppins - Used for headings and navigation.

Open Sans - Used for body text, forms, and general content.
